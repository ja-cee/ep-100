Designing the model on Fusion 360

I will be using my chess piece assignment as an example.

First, I downloaded a reference image from the internet and inserted it into Fusion 360

Next, I created a 2D sketch and traced the reference image. (The base is half done as it will be revolved later on)

The horse's head is extruded by pressing e on the keyboard and dragging the arrow outwards

On the other hand, the base of the chess piece is revolved.
