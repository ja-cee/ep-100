This website was created to document my progress in the Digital Fabrication & Prototyping Fundamentals(EP1000) module.

About EP1000: The elective is organised around a self-directed project where students develop design thinking and prototyping skills. Students will apply design and digital fabrication process for developing a functional prototype by the end of the module.

The topics covered in this module include web development, 2D and 3D modelling, and 3D printing.
